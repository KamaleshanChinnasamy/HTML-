OOTY

Ooty, also known as Udagamandalam, is a popular hill station located in the southern state of Tamil Nadu, India. It is situated in the Nilgiri Hills, which are part of the Western Ghats, and is known for its scenic beauty and pleasant climate. Here are some key highlights and information about Ooty. Ooty enjoys a temperate climate throughout the year, with cool temperatures even during the summer months. It's a great place to escape the heat and humidity of the plains. Ooty is renowned for its lush green landscapes, rolling hills, and tea plantations. The town is surrounded by dense forests, making it a paradise for nature lovers and photographers. The Nilgiri Mountain Railway, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, runs a charming toy train that connects Ooty to the town of Mettupalayam.
